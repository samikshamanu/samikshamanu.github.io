Samiksha K M

Profile

Samiksha K M is one of the leading bharatanatyam dancers of state Karnataka. Thumping into the dance world at an age as tender as 4, Samiksha has been mesmerizing the audience with her immaculate style and innate creativity.After initial tutelage under Ambale Rajeshwari and Unnath Jain, she is currently a disciple of Guru.Srikanth Natarajan.She also being a flautist, initially learnt under well-known flautist Vidwan.A.V.Prakash and is current learning under Vidwan.A.P.Krishna Prasad.

She has treaded the path of dance with a heart of an artist and the will of an innovator.She is currently persuing her B.E graduation from Government Engineering College,Hassan.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Completed vidwath examinations in bharatanatyam with distinction and stood 3rd rank in the Mysore region
Central government CCRT scholarship holder
Performed in different states of country
Honored by many prestigious institutions and stages
performed in Hampi Utsav, Mahamastakabhisheka, Hoysala mahotsava and many other prestigious stages
